Iphones

Iphones are a line of mobile phones developed and created by the company Apple and they run on the IOS operating system. There is a wide variation of them to get and each fit a purpose. Theres a budget option such as the iPhone 16e for £599 and a Pro option such as an iPhone 17 Pro which goes for £1,099.00.

Iphones are high quality and have great hardware attached. The Iphone 16 can even run Resident Evil 4 remake natively.

The iPhone 16e is £599. It has 3 storage options: 128GB, 256Gb and 512GB. It weights 167g, has a 6.1inch screen and is water resistant.

It has an A18 chip and records videos up to 4k 60fps and slow mo videos record at 1080p 120/240fps.
